Enter a word or phrase to search for in the descriptions.  Wildcards are permitted.
<br>
<ul>
<li><b>gout</b> Looks in the descriptions for the <u>word</u> "gout".  This would not find descriptions containing "gouty".
<li><b>gout*</b> Looks in the descriptions for the <u>word</u> "gout" or any word starting with "gout".  This would find "gouty".
